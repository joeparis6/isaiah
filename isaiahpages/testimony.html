My Testimony

Studying the book of Isaiah strengthened my Faith in Christ as the Lord and Savior of Israel. I testify that He is the Jehovah of ancient times that delivered Jerusalem and that he is the messiah that can deliver mankind from sin. I know that He is a divine warrior that can vanquish the evils and sicknesses in my life so that I might become a new creature. Through Him I can become holy and sanctified. I trust in His power so that I may be still even in times of great turmoil. I testify that the great redeemer of whom Isaiah spoke is the redeemer of all humankind and that He lives.
